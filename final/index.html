AI Hallucinations Dominate as the Key Challenge in Legal Cases

The Cost of Using AI in Courts Across America

By Sophia Burton

Photo Courtesy of Unsplash

Reporting Highlights

  • Out of a total 342 cases in the United States, 91% of them involved AI hallucinated cases and citations.
  • The majority of cases were flagged by courts that deemed AI usage was “implied.”
  • Of the 342 cases that occurred between June 2023 and October 2025, 299 of them occurred in 2025.
  • The highest monetary penalty was $31,100 for a case involving both false quotes and fabricated cases and citations.
